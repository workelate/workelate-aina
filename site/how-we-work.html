01

Agents do the volume, seniors do the judgment

Code generation, testing, documentation, data plumbing, report drafting, the 80% of delivery hours that juniors bill for, run on our agent fleet. Two or three senior people with agents now produce what 8–10 traditional engineers produce. The senior time goes where it matters: understanding your operation and making the calls.

02

We build one system at a time

No portfolio of 12 workstreams sharing a steering committee. One system, one accountable pair, weekly demos on your real data. Focus is the second-biggest speed multiplier after agents.

03

The verify loop: nobody grades their own homework

Every build runs inside a deterministic verification gate, automated checks that block "done" until the system provably works on your data. The same discipline applies to our agents in production: every action logged, every claim traceable to a source event. This is how a 2-person firm ships with enterprise-grade governance.

04

Fixed price, priced after diagnosis

The Diagnostic Sprint (2 weeks, fixed fee) produces the build plan with the price on it. We can price fixed because agents make our costs predictable, and because we've done the diagnosis, not estimated from a proposal template. If the Sprint finds nothing worth building, we tell you in writing.

05

The savings go to you

Removing the pyramid removes most of the cost. We keep senior-firm margins on a small team; you pay 3–4× less than an SI quote for the same outcome. For a $5–50M business that means a serious transformation build at $30K–$100K instead of a seven-figure program.

06

We run on what we sell

WE_AINA is WorkElate's own agency, our delivery runs on WorkElate, the AI-native operating system we build. Your engagement is managed, executed and audited on the same platform we ship to clients. If our product could not run a real agency at commercial stakes, we would be the first to know, and the first to pay for it.
