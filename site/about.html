About us

Small on purpose.
Senior by design.

WE_AINA is a digital transformation firm built the way we believe every consultancy will be built ten years from now: a small senior core, a specialist bench, and a fleet of agents doing the volume work. No pyramid. No juniors learning on your invoice.

Why "WE_AINA"

WorkElate's AI-Native Agency.
We run on the product we build.

WE_AINA is the agency arm of WorkElate, our AI-native operating system for agencies. Every engagement we deliver runs on WorkElate itself — the same platform, the same agents, the same audit trail we sell. That is deliberate: an agency that runs its own delivery on its own product has skin in the game no slide deck can fake. Our services revenue funds the company — bootstrapped, no burn clock — and every client engagement doubles as live proof that the product carries real work at commercial stakes.

When we say a workflow can run on agents, it is because ours already do.

What we believe

The convictions under every engagement

01

Receipts over trust

Every claim we make carries a number; every agent action carries a log line. If a consultancy asks you to trust its status report, ask who audits the reporter.

02

Transformation is sitting-in-the-room work

You cannot redesign an operation from an offshore delivery center. Diagnosis happens in your dispatch office, your studio, your finance room.

03

You own everything

Code, data, system, documentation. Lock-in is a business model, not an accident — and it isn't ours.

04

Honesty is cheaper than churn

If the Sprint finds nothing worth building, we say so in writing. A client who trusts the "no" comes back with the next real problem.
