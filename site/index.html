06 · How engagement works

Three phases. Fixed scope. Receipts at every step.

Weeks 1–2

Diagnostic Sprint

We sit inside your operation, map where hours and money leak, and hand you a build plan with numbers on it, including the fixed price of the build. If we find nothing worth building, we say so in writing.

Weeks 3–10

Build

One system, shipped against the plan at the fixed price from the Sprint, typically $30K–$100K depending on scope. Weekly demos on your real data, not slides. You own the code and the data, no lock-in clause buried on page 14.
